Meet the Mighty Ones

No less than one sixth of Language Strides LLC's services are provided free or at reduced rates to local families in financial need. The Mighty Ones are the children who benefit from these services–Unstoppable kids who are determined to learn.

Physician and community referrals are thanked with a donation to Cornerstones, a nonprofit organization in Reston, Virginia. Cornerstones promotes self-sufficiency by providing support and advocacy for those in need of food, shelter, affordable housing, quality childcare, and other human services.





Language Strides LLC proudly serves the communities of Reston and Herndon, Virginia and the surrounding area.
